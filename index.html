CMOS-SALINE NEURAL REGENERATION FORMULA

DIY Protocol – Full Instructions

⚙️ TOOLS YOU'LL NEED:

  • Blender or glass jar with lid
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Glass storage container (32 oz recommended)
  • Stove and pot for boiling water

🌿 INGREDIENTS (Per 32 oz / ~1 liter batch)

Base:

  • Wildcrafted Sea Moss (raw): 1/4 cup dry (about 10–15g)
  • Distilled Water: 2.5 cups (600 ml total)
  • Sea Salt (non-iodized or pink salt): 1/2 tsp
  • Raw Honey (optional): 1 tsp (improves absorption)

Herbal Extract Powder Blend (1/2 tsp each):

  • Ashwagandha
  • Lion's Mane
  • Bacopa Monnieri
  • Ginkgo Biloba
  • Mucuna Pruriens
  • Gotu Kola
  • Turmeric
  • Black Pepper (optional): pinch (for turmeric absorption)

💧 USAGE

  • Daily oral dose: 1 tablespoon (15ml) once or twice daily
  • Optional topical use: dab onto temples, neck, or scalp
  • Optional vapor use: mix 1 tablespoon into water in a humidifier or diffuser for light inhalation

⏳ SHELF LIFE

  • Refrigerated: Up to 3 weeks
  • Freeze in cubes for longer storage

📜 STEP-BY-STEP INSTRUCTIONS

1 Step 1: Clean and Prep Sea Moss

  1. Rinse 1/4 cup dry sea moss under cold water to remove sand/debris.
  2. Soak in a bowl with fresh water for 12–24 hours (no heat). It will expand.

2 Step 2: Make the Saline Base

  1. In a pot, warm 2.5 cups distilled water.
  2. Add 1/2 tsp sea salt. Stir until fully dissolved.
  3. Let cool to room temp.

3 Step 3: Blend the Gel

  1. Drain soaked sea moss. Add to blender.
  2. Pour in 2 cups of your saline water.
  3. Blend until smooth (1–2 mins). Add more saline as needed for texture.
  4. Add 1 tsp honey (optional). Blend again.

4 Step 4: Add Herbs

  1. Once blended, stir in your powdered herbs: 1/2 tsp each of Ashwagandha, Lion's Mane, Bacopa, Ginkgo, Mucuna, Gotu Kola, Turmeric.
  2. Add a pinch of black pepper if using turmeric.
  3. Mix until fully integrated.
  4. Store in glass container. Keep refrigerated.
